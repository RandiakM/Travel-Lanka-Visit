Day 1 - Kandy City

The cultural capital of Sri Lanka, the Kandy city is full of history, culture and unique personality. The city is surrounded by some majestic mountains and it attractive touring destinations for the travelers, who want to enjoy the natural beauty of this island along with exploring the rich and colorful culture.
This city is also situated very close to the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage and visiting this place will be a unique experience for the travelers.
Because this place is the only place in the whole world where you can help the elephants for bathing and feed along with this, you can even get engaged in working for their rehabilitation.

Activities

● Arrival to Sri Lanka (Bandaranayaka International Airport) in Katunayaka
● Proceed to Kandy city
● En route, visit Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage
● Kandy city tour (Upper lake drive, Gem & Bathik shopping, Oak Ray wood carvings)
● Witness a cultural dance in the evening
● Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 2 - Nuwara Eliya City

The city of Nuwara Eliya is also known as the 'Little England', for the weather it has as well as the amazing colonial architecture and buildings which are seen all over the city.This hill town has plenty to offer you that surely leave you breathless.
This is just some of the marvelous places you can visit in the event you decide to stay here.

Activities

● After breakfast at the hotel, visit Temple of Tooth & participate in the morning ceremony
● Proceed to Nuwaraeliya known as Little England
● En route, Tea Plantation & Tea Factory visit
● Visit beautiful waterfalls in Hill country
● Take Nuwaraeliya city Tour (Strawberry farm visit, boat ride can be arranged)
● Check in to the hotel and overnight Stay

Day 3 - Yala National Park

The Yala National Park is a popular and world renowned wildlife reservation in the Sri Lanka. It is most notable renowned for being home to the attractive and elusive Sri Lankan leopard. Despite their elusive nature, almost all of the travelers will certainly identify a leopard in this stunning place because of the high attention of leopard residing in the park.

Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel proceed to Yala national park
● Take a 4x4 Jeep safari in Yala National Park in the afternoon or next day early morning
● Check in to the hotel & relax and overnight stay

Day 4 - Bentota & Galle

The amazing Bentota is situated 200 km away from the Equator and very near to the Southern tip of the Island of Sri Lanka. T
he eye-catching beauty of this bright and sandy beach will transform your dreams and visions of a tropical heaven into an everyday reality.
This breath-taking beach is crescent shaped and it plenty of natural beauties to offer you as this is an ideal place for just sitting and relaxing.
And you will forget about the rusty life that is far away from you.

Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Bentota
● Galle City tour (2 hours)
● Engage in water sports
● Evening enjoy the sun set and relax at the beach
● Overnight stay at the Hotel

Day 5 - Colombo City

The city of Colombo is the commercial capital of Sri Lanka and it holds together the new and old trend of the county very beautifully.
The city also represents the colonial remnants with high-rise hotels and commercial banks. Additionally, Colombo was the eighth-century port and it houses several financial, administrative, and other major institutions that are of relevance to the travelers. Right now, the city has been developed into a modern city and boasts the amazing blend of Sri Lanka's ethnic race and religious people.

Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Colombo
● On the way you get to visit a turtles hatchery in Kosgoda
● Check in to the hotel & relax at the hotel
● Later proceed with a Colombo Shopping and City Tour
