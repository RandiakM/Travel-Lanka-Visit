Day 1 - Polonaruwa City

The SuduAraliya Hotel in Polonnaruwa is located close to the giant lake of "Parakrama Samudraya." Moreover, the hotel has a talented group of chefs for cooking eastern, western, and local dishes. If it isn't enough, you should also visit the Wasgamuwa and Kawudulla parks to experience the wildlife and rare breed of birds.

Day 2 - Sigiriya / Dambulla

Dambulla is to be found in the central province of the island and the city is home to a number of spectacular attractions. In addition, the city has the largest rose quartz mountain range in the entire Asia and the ironwood forest known as NamalUyana. Now, the big quartz rose mountain is millions of years old and it encircled by the ancient ironwood forest trees. Also, the grand rock fortress of Sigiriya is another major attraction, which is worth checking.

Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Dambulla
● Dambulla city tour and visit Dambulla cave temple (Golden Temple & Golden Buddha Statue)
● Check-in to the hotel & relax
● Evening proceed to Sri Lankan Village walk
● Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 3 - Sigiriya / Dambulla

In this day, Sigiriya will offer the visitors to witness the 5th-century paintings that are completed by unrivalled landscaped gardens. The royal pavilion is 600 feet long and the view from the top is breathtaking. In addition, the construction of the attractions is both symmetric and asymmetric. They are also the prime examples of the 5th-century art and architecture of Sri Lanka as well. All in all, Sigiriya is considered as a goldmine for the art lovers and archeologists.


Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel, take an ancient city tour in Sigiriya
● Visit most famous Sigiriya Rock Fortress - 1200 steps
● Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 4 - Kandy City

Kandy is an ancient city that is located in the center of Sri Lanka. The city is the cultural capital of Sri Lanka and a prime example of rich cultural heritage. Additionally, the city lies in the hills and it is regarded as a relaxing and reflecting city. Here, you will some major attractions in the form of the tooth relic of the Lord Buddha, the famous 'Dalada Maligawa' or 'Temple of the Tooth.' Apart from these, the city also boasts many other landmarks along with ancient temples including the Peradeniya Botanical Gardens.


Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel, Proceed to Kandy
● En route, visit Matale Spice garden & take a free herbal massage (Head, Shoulder or feet)
● Arrival in Kandy & take a city tour(Upper lake drive, Gem &Bathik shopping, Oak Ray wood carvings)
● Check in to the hotel ● Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 6 - Nuwara Eliya

Nuwara Eliya is dubbed as the garden city of Asia for its marvelous valleys, hills and waterfalls. It is standing 6,182 feet above the sea level and it is to be found at the foot of Mount Pidurutalagala, Sri Lanka's highest peak. Apart from these, the Hakgala gardens grown tea plantations and the Worlds End at Horton Plains are also fantastic tourist's attractions where visitors come in big numbers.

Day 8 - Yala National Park

The Yala National Park is the most popular wildlife sanctuary in Sri Lanka. This is a big wildlife reserve where a number of unbelievable wildlife creatures and plants live day-by-day. This wildlife sanctuary is among the best places to spot the elusive leopard and an array of endemic birds is also there to be seen.
