Day 1 - Sigiriya / Dambulla

The beautiful Sigiriya comes up with a full gallery of fifth-century paintings and canvas finished with landscape gardens that are certainly matchless by any modern fortress in the entire world. The eye-catching sights from the 600 feet higher royal pavilion sis surely capture your heart and mind for a long period of time. Sri Lanka's most colorful history as well as the art and architecture of fifth century is just reflecting by the constructions of the symmetric and asymmetric buildings along with the principle that is used as a part of the design for the gardens. Besides that, the tropical wood was mostly used for the construction of the pavilions in addition to the other different buildings.

Activities

● Arrival to Sri Lanka (Bandaranayaka International Airport) in Katunayaka and proceed to Sigiriya
● Visit Pinawala Elephant orphanage
● Check-in to the hotel & relax
● Sigiriya rock fortress
● Evening village walk
● Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 2 - Sigiriya / Dambulla

The Dambulla town is situated in the Matale district of the Sri Lanka. This city has a great deal of historical as well as cultural significance as it is well known for being home to several ancient sanctuaries like the Dambulla cave temple complex, which is the biggest and most well conserved in the whole Sri Lanka.The visitors can also visit the 'Na Uyana Arana' or the 'Ironwood Grove Forest Monastery'. Apart from this, the voyagers can also experience the natural village life, taste some mouth-watering village food and visit numerous destinations of Sri Lanka's social triangle.

Activities

● Visit Pinawala Elephant orphanage
● Check-in to the hotel & relax
● Sigiriya rock fortress
● Evening village walk
● Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 3 - Kandy City

Located in the central of the Sri Lanka, the city of Kandy is known as the cultural capital of this country. This beautiful city is renowned for the home to many ancient temples like the 'Temple of The Tooth' where the holy tooth relic of the Lord Buddha is retained.This city is the perfect place for just sitting and reflecting. Apart from this, the city also comprises the Peradeniya Botanical Gardens, the large of its kind in the whole island.


Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel, Proceed to Kandy
● En route, visit Matale Spice garden & take a free herbal massage & taste a free herbal massage
● Arrival in Kandy & take a city tour (Upper lake drive)
● Check in to the hotel
● Witness a cultural dance in the evening
● Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 4 - Nuwara Eliya

The Nuwara Eliya city is the highest city of the whole Sri Lanka as it is situated 6,182 feet high from the sea level in addition to the foot of Mount Pidurutalagala. This city is known as the garden city of the Asia as is has been home to numerous eye-catching hills, waterfalls and valleys. The visitors come to this place for enjoying the climate, visit a park, playing golf, visit the amazing tea plantation etc. You can also visit the Hakgala gardens, the Horton Plains or the Worlds End, where there is a sudden drop of a few thousand feet in the territory.


Activities

● After breakfast at the hotel, visit Temple of Tooth & participate in the morning ceremony
● Proceed to Nuwaraeliya known as Little England
● En route, Tea Plantation & Tea Factory visit
● Visit beautiful waterfalls in Hill country
● Take Nuwara Eliya city Tour (Strawberry farm visit, boat ride can be arranged with additional cost )
● Check in to the hotel & relax
● Overnight Stay at the hotel

Day 5 - Yala

The Yala National Park is well known for the best wildlife safari in the whole Sri Lanka. This place is being home to the plenty of endemic and No-endemic animals just like the elusive Sri Lankan leopard, the mighty Sri Lankan elephant, bears, peacocks, deer etc.


Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel proceed to Yala
● Take a 4x4 Jeep safari in Yala National Park in the afternoon or next day early morning
● Check in to the hotel & relax
● Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 6 - Bentota

The Bentota is located just 200 km away from the Equator along with it is situated very close to the Southern tip of the Sri Lanka Island. Besides, this place is a perfect destination for just to sit and relax as you will forget the rustiness of your other life that is far away from you. Apart from that, this tropical sandy beach comes with the best sunsets and sunrises of the whole county.


Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Bentota
● Madu Gaga boat ride can be arrange with additional cost
● Galle City tour
● Check in to the hotel & relax at the hotel

Day 7 - Bentota

The city of Bentota is located southern shoreline of the Sri Lanka. This city has comes with some unique as well as breath-taking experiences for the visitors who are the nature lovers as well as the excitement lovers. The city is renowned for being home to the beautiful golden sandy beaches, a tropical lagoon in addition to the Madu River. This place is an ideal place for families as well as the lone visitors and the couples, who will all find something that suits their requirements.


Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel leisure & relax in the beach
● Overnight stay at the hotel

Day 8 - Colombo

The commercial capital of Sri Lanka, Colombo city has plenty of gifts to offer you that will leave you breathless. This city is being very popular for holding the old and new culture as well as the history of the country very carefully. Besides, this city also comes with the exciting nightlife as well as some world class restaurants, which attracts numerous people every year in this city. Furthermore, this city has been the eight-century port, which houses different administrative and financial offices that are relevant to the travelers. Also, these offices are considered as the finest part of the colonial structures.


Activities

● After Breakfast at the hotel, proceed to Colombo
● Check in to the hotel & relax at the hotel
● Later proceed with a Colombo Shopping and City Tour
● Overnight stay at the hotel
