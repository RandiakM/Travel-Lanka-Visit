Day 1 - Kandy City

The Kandy city is situated in the central of the Sri Lanka and it is the socio-cultural capital of the country.
Settled in the middle of a glorious mountain, the Kandy city is a delightful and holiday destination for the travelers who want to experience the natural beauty as well as it is rich and bright culture of the island.
Travelers can also tour the Royal Botanical Gardens in Peradeniya; the largest of its genre in the whole island along with the garden is been home for more than 4000 species of various plants.
The Kandy city is also situated in close proximity to the Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage.
The city holds numerous cultural and historical treasures that you would be able to visit on the off chance that you decide to spend some energy and time in this magnificent city.

Activities

● Arrival to Sri Lanka (Bandaranayaka International Airport) in Katunayaka
● Meet & greet with our representatives at the airport and proceed to Kandy
● En route Visit Pinnawala elephant orphanage - Entrance Included
● Arrival in Kandy & take a city tour (Upper lake drive)
● Witness a cultural dance in the evening - Entrance Included

Day 2 - Colombo City

Being the capital city of Sri Lanka, Colombo receives a lot of attraction from the visitors. It is the center of commercial and cultural activities of the island.
This marvelous and stirring city houses numerous treasures along with well-kept secrets. There are plenty of shopping choices in this island.The Colombo city also home to several high-quality restaurants serving plenty of international and local cuisine.
Furthermore, if you enjoy your life through partying, Colombo comes up with flourishing nightlife scene with many night clubs, bars and regular shoreline parties that will certainly keep you on your feet and moving to the beat.

Day 3 - Bentota (Beach Area)

Bentota and its breathtaking sandy beach pretty much transform your dreams and visions of a tropical paradise into an everyday reality. Located close to the Southern tip of the Island of Sri Lanka and only about 200 km from the Equator, this secluded crescent shaped beach is the perfect place to sit back, relax and forget about all the hustle and bustle of your other life that's a million miles away.
